# The gap between a working project and a sellable product

## Body

AI Workstation already worked technically. That did not mean a customer could buy it.

The commercial gaps were simple but important:

- a 30-second explanation;
- a real-device demo;
- a visible price;
- a defined delivery scope;
- an FAQ;
- a clear contact path.

This is a useful reminder for technical founders: product quality includes reducing uncertainty before the customer ever speaks to you.

Tonight’s work focused on those assets rather than another feature. The next milestone is not a larger backlog—it is the first real user conversation.
