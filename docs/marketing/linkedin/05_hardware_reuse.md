# Giving old hardware a focused second life

## Body

Repurposing hardware works best when the new job matches the device’s strengths.

A Kindle is not a good general-purpose monitor. It is excellent at displaying stable, high-contrast information with very low power use. That makes it well suited to a personal AI operations dashboard.

AI Workstation uses the device for one clear responsibility: keep AI-agent status visible while the main workstation remains focused on creation.

The lesson extends beyond Kindle: sustainable hardware reuse is not about forcing old devices to imitate new ones. It is about finding a narrow role where their constraints become advantages.
