# Looking for early users of a Kindle AI command center

## Body

I am looking for a narrow group of early users:

- you run two or more AI coding agents;
- you occasionally miss completion or approval states;
- you have an unused Kindle or want a quiet status display;
- you value local operation.

AI Workstation monitors Claude Code, OpenAI Codex, MiMo Code, selected quota, and balances on an e-ink dashboard.

The project is open source. Personal Setup is available for users who want help getting a working device, and custom workflow services are available for more complex use cases.

Demo: https://baobateer-arch.github.io/AI-Workstation/
